Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy, and comforting breakfast favorites. They’re quick to make and super versatile—enjoy them with syrup, fruits, chocolate chips, or even just butter. Perfect for a cozy morning meal.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Melted butter
  • Optional: Vanilla extract, syrup, fruit, chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry mixtures—don’t overmix.
  4. Heat a pan and grease it lightly.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
