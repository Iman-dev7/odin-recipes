Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar Salad is a light, crunchy salad made with fresh romaine lettuce, crispy croutons, and a creamy Caesar dressing. It’s often topped with Parmesan cheese and sometimes grilled chicken or anchovies. It’s a popular starter or light meal known for its bold, tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optional: Grilled chicken, anchovies

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce.
  2. Toss it in a bowl with Caesar dressing.
  3. Add croutons and Parmesan.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Top with grilled chicken or anchovies if you like, then serve.
