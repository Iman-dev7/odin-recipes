lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese. The hearty filling typically includes seasoned ground beef or pork simmered in tomato sauce, complemented by herbs like basil and oregano for extra flavor. Each layer is carefully stacked to create a delicious, satisfying bite that’s both cheesy and savory. Once assembled, it's baked to golden perfection, resulting in a bubbling, flavorful dish that's perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna nodles
  • 1 lb ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the noodles
  2. Cook the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Top with mozzarella
  6. Bake
  7. Cool and serve
